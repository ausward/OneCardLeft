# One Card Left

A web-based implementation of the classic card game UNO. Play interactively in your browser with dynamic card rendering and game mechanics.

## Features

- **Interactive Gameplay**: Play UNO directly in your web browser
- **Game Mechanics**: Includes special cards like Skip, Reverse, Draw Two, Wild, and Wild Draw Four
- **Responsive Design**: Full viewport gameplay experience

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Play your cards by clicking on them
3. Match the color or number of the card on the top of the discard pile
4. Use special cards strategically to outmaneuver your opponents
5. Be the first to empty your hand and shout "One Card Left!"

## Card Types

- **Number Cards (0-9)**: Regular cards in four colors (Red, Blue, Green, Yellow)
- **Skip**: Skip the next player's turn
- **Reverse**: Reverse the direction of play
- **Draw Two**: The next player draws two cards
- **Wild**: Change the color to any you choose
- **Wild Draw Four**: Change the color and the next player draws four cards

## Technologies Used

- HTML5
- CSS3
- Vanilla JavaScript
